Life's challenges can feel overwhelming and isolating at times. You might be struggling with anxiety or self-doubt, stuck in patterns that no longer serve you, or noticing how past traumas continue to impact the way you connect with others. You're ready for something to change but may be unsure of what step to take next.

This is where our work together begins. I'm committed to creating a warm, supportive space where you can show up as your complete, authentic self. My goal is to truly understand you and your story in all its complexity.

Approach

You are the expert on your own life, and I'm here to listen deeply, reflect with care, and walk alongside you as you make meaning of your past and envision new possibilities for the future. Together, we'll clarify your vision for growth, connection, and meaningful change. From there, we'll co-create a path forward.

Therapy, much like life itself, holds the full spectrum of human emotion—and yes, that includes moments of genuine laughter and lightness alongside the deeper work. I believe there's room for humor and joy even as we navigate the difficult terrain together.

I draw from an integrative framework including person-centered, relational, psychodynamic, somatic, feminist, and multicultural approaches. My work is strength-based and honors your lived experience.
