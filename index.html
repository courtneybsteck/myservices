Life's challenges can feel overwhelming and isolating. You might be struggling emotionally, navigating a difficult transition, or feeling stuck in patterns that no longer serve you. Perhaps the echoes of past experiences or traumas are shaping how you connect with others. You're ready for something to change, even if the next step feels uncertain. This is where our work together begins.

I am committed to creating a warm, supportive space where you can show up as your complete, authentic self. My goal is to truly understand your story in all its depth and complexity. Together, we'll carve out a new path toward meaningful, lasting change.
